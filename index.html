About PastorPro

Version 2.0.0 - Complete UI/UX Overhaul

Release Date: October 1, 2025

PastorPro is a comprehensive suite of ministry tools designed specifically for pastors and church leaders. Our mission is to help you manage your pastoral responsibilities more effectively, allowing you to focus on what matters most - shepherding your congregation and serving your community.

Our latest major update brings significant improvements to the user interface, user experience, and overall app architecture:

Modern Theme System

Professional Navy Blue and Sky Blue palette with Material 3 Design implementation throughout the app. Consistent styling for all components and dark mode ready architecture.

Enhanced Authentication

Modern login screen, user registration flow, remember me functionality, and password recovery. We've also fixed the logout error that was causing app crashes.

Improved Navigation

New navigation pattern with Home and Profile tabs for easy access to all features. State preservation when switching between tabs and login prompts for protected features.

Whether you're preparing sermons, managing church events, tracking member engagement, or organizing outreach programs, PastorPro provides the digital tools you need to streamline your ministry operations.

Features

Discover how PastorPro helps you simplify your ministry work with these powerful features:

Mission-Based Structure

Organize your ministry work by missions, regions, and districts. Create hierarchical relationships between missions and departments for better organization.

Activities & Appointments

Track pastoral activities, appointments, and events in a unified calendar. Manage your schedule effectively with reminders and notifications.

Department Management

Manage church departments with customizable forms and reports. Create, edit, and organize departments across different missions.

User & Role Management

Control access with detailed user roles and permissions. Assign administrators and manage team members across missions and departments.

Modern Dashboard

Access all features through an intuitive, responsive dashboard. Enjoy a beautiful UI with lazy authentication for better user experience.

Task Management

Create, assign, and track tasks related to your ministry. Set priorities, due dates, and monitor progress with the integrated todo system.

Reports & Analytics

Generate comprehensive reports of ministry activities. Export data for presentations and analysis to support strategic planning.

Offline Functionality

Access critical features even without internet connection. Data synchronizes automatically when connectivity is restored.
